## App Store Description (English)

### Short Description (80 characters)
Get daily questions, vote with friends, and discover surprising opinions!

### Full Description

**Daily Questions That Bring Your Group Together**

dontAskUs is the fun, simple way to connect with your friends through daily questions. Every day, you and your group get a new question to answer – from lighthearted polls to thought-provoking dilemmas.

**🎯 HOW IT WORKS**
• Create or join a group with your friends
• Answer a new question every day
• See what your friends voted for
• Build your answer streak

**✨ FEATURES**
• **5 Question Types** - Yes/No polls, multiple choice, free text, vote for a friend, or pick a duo
• **Real-time Results** - Watch the votes come in live
• **Answer Streaks** - Keep your streak alive by answering daily
• **Group Leaderboards** - See who has the longest streak
• **Dark Mode** - Easy on the eyes, day or night
• **Multiple Groups** - Be part of several friend groups at once

**🔒 PRIVACY FIRST**
• No ads, no tracking
• Your data stays with your group
• Simple, secure authentication

**👥 PERFECT FOR**
• Friend groups wanting to stay connected
• Teams looking for fun daily interactions
• Couples wanting conversation starters
• Anyone who loves polls and opinions!

Download dontAskUs now and discover what your friends really think!
