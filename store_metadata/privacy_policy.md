# Privacy Policy for dontAskUs

**Last Updated:** January 4, 2026

## Introduction

Welcome to dontAskUs ("we", "our", or "us"). We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide
- **Display Name:** The name you choose to display within your groups
- **Avatar Color:** Your selected avatar color preference
- **Answers:** Your responses to daily questions
- **Group Membership:** Which groups you belong to

### Information Collected Automatically
- **Session Tokens:** Encrypted tokens for authentication
- **App Usage Data:** Anonymous usage statistics to improve the app
- **Device Information:** Device type and operating system version (for compatibility)

## How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:
- Provide and maintain the dontAskUs service
- Enable you to participate in groups and answer questions
- Display your answers and voting results to group members
- Calculate and display answer streaks
- Improve and optimize the app experience

## Information Sharing

### Within Your Groups
- Your display name, avatar color, and answers are visible to other members of your groups
- Your answer streak is visible to group members

### We Do NOT
- Sell your personal information to third parties
- Share your data with advertisers
- Use your data for targeted advertising
- Access the content of your answers outside of displaying them to group members

## Data Storage and Security

- Your data is stored securely on our servers
- Session tokens are encrypted on your device using secure storage
- We use industry-standard security measures to protect your data
- Data is transmitted using HTTPS encryption

## Data Retention

- Your account data is retained while you are an active member of groups
- When you leave a group, your answers remain visible to that group
- You can request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- **Access:** Request a copy of your personal data
- **Correction:** Request correction of inaccurate data
- **Deletion:** Request deletion of your account and data
- **Portability:** Request your data in a portable format
- **Withdraw Consent:** Stop using the app at any time

## Children's Privacy

dontAskUs is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:
- Posting the new Privacy Policy in the app
- Updating the "Last Updated" date

## Contact Us
